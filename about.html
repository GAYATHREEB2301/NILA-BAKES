Our Story

Learn about the passion and craftsmanship behind NILA BAKES.

10+ Years of Love

From My Kitchen, With Love

NILA BAKES wasn’t born in a factory or a high-end commercial building. It began in the heart of my home—a dusty kitchen filled with the scent of vanilla and the sound of my twin daughters laughing while they tried to "help" with the spatula.

What started as a hobby—baking simple tea cakes for friends—soon turned into a passion that I couldn't ignore. I remember the very first order I received from a neighbor for a daughter's first birthday. I was nervous, excited, and stayed up until 3 AM ensuring every petal of the rose frosting was perfect.

Today, NILA BAKES has grown beyond my home kitchen, but the philosophy remains the same: handcrafted quality over commercial quantity. We still source our butter from local farms and our chocolate from ethical suppliers because we believe the soul of a cake lies in its ingredients.
